# CRM Documentation Knowledge Base

## 1. Salesforce CRM
**Overview:** Salesforce is a leading enterprise CRM offering cloud-based tools for sales, service, marketing, and analytics.
**Key Features:**
- Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud
- Einstein AI for predictive insights
- AppExchange integrations
**API Info:**
- REST API: https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/
- Authentication: OAuth 2.0
**Best For:** Enterprises needing customization and scalability.
**Pricing:** Starts $25/user/month
---

## 2. HubSpot CRM
**Overview:** HubSpot provides an all-in-one CRM with marketing, sales, and service tools.
**Key Features:**
- Free CRM core
- Automation workflows
- Integrates with Gmail, Outlook, Zapier
**API Info:**
- REST API: https://developers.hubspot.com/docs/api/overview
- Authentication: OAuth 2.0 or API Key
**Best For:** SMBs and startups looking for ease of use.
**Pricing:** Free tier; paid from $45/month
---

## üîÑ CRM Comparisons

### Salesforce vs HubSpot
- **Ease of Use:** HubSpot easier; Salesforce steeper learning curve.
- **Customization:** Salesforce far more customizable.
- **Pricing:** HubSpot cheaper for startups; Salesforce better for scaling.
- **Integrations:** Both strong, Salesforce wider enterprise reach.

### Zoho vs HubSpot
- **Pricing:** Zoho cheaper overall.
- **Features:** HubSpot has stronger marketing suite; Zoho more automation flexibility.
- **Ease of Use:** HubSpot simpler UI.

### Pipedrive vs Freshsales
- **Focus:** Pipedrive = sales pipeline; Freshsales = service automation.
- **AI:** Freshsales includes AI scoring; Pipedrive limited.
- **Integrations:** Both support popular email and calendar tools.
